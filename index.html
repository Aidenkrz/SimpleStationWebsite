SimpleStation is a Space Station 14 community intended to bring people together and to make the game a little more enjoyable.
We offer server hosting, develop useful tools, and maintain an active community more than happy to help.

Below you'll find SimpleStation's collection of 'in-house' servers, each offering something different. For a larger collection, you can find our 'community servers' on the Community Servers page. If you're interested in getting hosted and becoming a Community Server yourself or would simply like to learn more about development and contributing, feel free to message @pspritechologist on the SimpleStation Discord server!
